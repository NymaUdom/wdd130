Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to provide users with a seamless, personalized, and efficient shopping experience that enhances convenience, reduces waste, and fosters informed purchasing decision.

Audience

  • Households: Families and individuals responsible for grocery shopping and household supplies.
  • Businesses: Small businesses or offices that need to manage office supply procurement and inventory.
  • Tech-Savvy Shoppers: Users who are comfortable with mobile apps and technology, looking for a more convenient shopping experience.
  • Eco-conscious Consumers: People who prioritize sustainability and are interested in making environmentally-friendly choices while shopping.
  • Budget-Conscious Shoppers: Users who want to manage their expenses and stick to a budget while shopping.
  • Busy Professionals: Individuals with busy schedules who want to streamline their shopping and reduce time spent on errands.
  • Online Shoppers: Users who frequently shop online and want to integrate their online purchases with in-store needs.
  • Sustainability Advocates: People who are concerned about reducing waste and would benefit from alerts about expiring items to minimize food waste.
  • Collaborative Shoppers: Households or groups of people who share shopping responsibilities and need a way to coordinate lists.
